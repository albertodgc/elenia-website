Elenia Design System
The complete design language for Elenia, Colombia's #1 WhatsApp recepcionista for local businesses. Use this as a reference for building any brand touchpoint: landing pages, dashboards, emails, social media, print materials.
Brand Identity
NameElenia: a warm, human name. She is the recepcionista: the friendly presence that answers, attends, and never sleeps.
TaglineTu recepcionista por WhatsApp
OriginHecho con orgullo desde Pereira, el corazón del eje cafetero 🇨🇴
MarketLocal service businesses across Colombia (beauty, wellness, barber, professional)
PositionLa recepcionista #1 de Colombia por WhatsApp
Price$99.000 COP/mes: fixed, all-inclusive, no hidden fees
Key differentiatorWorks 24/7: noches, domingos y festivos. Never says "AI" to customers.

Voice & Tone
Elenia speaks like a trusted friend who happens to know about business. Warm, direct, Colombian, and never corporate.
PrincipleDoDon't
Direct & warm"Tus clientes reciben respuesta en segundos""Nuestra plataforma proporciona respuestas automatizadas"
Colombian Spanish"por la tardecita", "le queda bien", "porfa"Spain Spanish or overly formal Latin American
Results over features"Te levantas con citas nuevas agendadas""Sistema de agendamiento integrado con notificaciones push"
Concrete numbers"Las inasistencias bajan hasta un 45%""Reduce significativamente las inasistencias"
Second person (usted/tú)"Tu negocio atendido", "Esto es para usted""Los usuarios pueden configurar..."
Never say "AI""recepcionista", "recepcionista virtual", "tu empleada""inteligencia artificial", "IA", "AI", "bot", "chatbot"

Colombian Context Rules
CurrencyAlways display in Colombian pesos with period as thousands separator: $99.000, $45.000, $65.000
HolidaysReference "festivos" (Colombian public holidays), Semana Santa, Navidad
Time format12-hour with "a.m." and "p.m." (Colombian convention)
Address styleUse "usted" for formal/respect, "tu" for casual; mix both naturally as Colombians do
Legal"Tratamiento de datos" (Colombian data protection language, per Ley 1581)
